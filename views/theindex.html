About Meego
Meego is a small, portable device with a mounting feature to allow for convenient immobilization of any personal belongings. When motion-triggered, it sets off an alarm and notifies your mobile device.
For the Student
Use Meego in the library. Step away to grab a snack or use the bathroom without packing away all your valuables and losing your study spot.
For the Professional
Use Meego in shared work spaces. Keep your valuable data and equipment safe when you go for a quick lunch or coffee trip.
Features
Motion-Activated Alarm
Meego's alarm is motion activated, so that any thief is quickly deterred as the sound attracts public attention at the same time.
Meego pairs with your smartphone and alerts you right away.
Smartphone Bluetooth Pairing
GPS Tracking
It's GPS enabled, so you always know where to find Meego.
